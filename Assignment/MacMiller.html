MacMiller

    Here's some his Achievements in music:
  • In 2010, he signed a record deal with independent label Rostrum Records and released his breakthrough mixtapes K.I.D.S. (2010) and Best Day Ever (2011).
  • Miller's debut studio album, Blue Slide Park (2011), became the first independently distributed debut album to top the US Billboard 200 since 1995.
  • After his death in September 2018, the single "Self Care" rose to number 33 on the Billboard Hot 100, his highest peak as a lead artist at the time.
  • Swimming was nominated for Best Rap Album at the 61st Annual Grammy Awards.
  • "Good News", became his highest-charting song as lead artist, peaking at number 17 on the Billboard Hot 100.
